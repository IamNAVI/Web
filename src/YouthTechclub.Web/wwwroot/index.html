Young people. IT industry. Now.

Youth Techclub connects young people with information technology.

We bring together individuals, companies and educational institutions.

OUR MISSION

Encourage young people to gain knowledge about IT.

HOW WE DO IT

We connect them in one network with industry and learning facilities.

We learn by doing – we define project idea or a problem and then try to solve it. Members attend regular meet-up’s or training sessions once in a one or two weeks. Coding is not the only thing we learn – we also do a lot of prototyping, graphic design and electronics. Technologies/methodologies we are exploring and plan to explore: C#, Javascript, ASP.NET, Azure, ORM, Bootstrap, jQuery, AngularJS, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Raspberry Pi, Xamarin (Android, Windows Phone), mobile app development, Agile.
